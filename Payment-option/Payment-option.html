payment plans &
financing options

We offer easy payment plans and financing options through Cherry, CareCredit, and Proceed Financial to fit your family’s needs.

With Cherry, you can split your treatment costs into smaller monthly installments — often with 0% APR and no hidden fees. Approvals are fast and flexible, with financing available up to $35,000 and no hard credit checks. Learn more about Cherry Payment Plans.

CareCredit is a healthcare credit card designed to help finance your dental care needs, allowing you to get the treatment you need when you need it. With no-interest financing options, CareCredit makes it simple to pay off more expensive treatments in a way that fits your budget.

Proceed Finance offers specialized dental and healthcare financing solutions, providing patients with the flexibility to manage large treatment costs. With competitive interest rates and terms of up to 12 years, they enable accessible and affordable monthly payments, ensuring patients can receive necessary care without financial strain.

Interested in learning more or applying for these third-party financing options? Visit the CareCredit website or the Proceed Finance website to apply and see if you qualify. Or contact us with questions at (816) 578-8342.

payment
information

Our team will always provide you with the cost of treatment beforehand. At that time, we can also discuss your payment options or financing needs, based on your situation.

For those with dental insurance, we do not always know in advance what services (if any) your insurance company will cover. All insurance companies and plans vary on what they will or will not cover. We can only guess based on what other patients’ insurance companies have done in the past.

Our Insurance Coordinator is happy to help in any way she can to help you know the exact cost to expect.
